Work Experience

Kazzicom

I was the sole designer at Kazzicom, an ERP (enterprise and resource) & IT consultancy, for three years. This meant I had responsibility in owning all design activity at the company.

My work mostly featured in the IT side of the business where I was responsible for the experiences of multiple websites and mobile applications. Within these digital- led initiatives, I used Adobe XD predominantly to craft lo and hi fidelity wireframes. Additionally, I designed a variety of promotional materials for Kazzicom and their clients such as flyers, posters, business cards and logos.

For each design project, I conversed continually with clients to make sure I was capturing their aspirations and more. Capturing feedback was also something I did regularly to help me in my development as well as to build relationships with our customers.
